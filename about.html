Privacy Policy

📅 Last updated: January 2026

Student GPA & Marks Calculator ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our web application.

📋 Data Collection

100% Local Storage • No Servers

We do NOT collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All information you enter (subjects, marks, GPA results, percentage calculations) is stored exclusively on your own device using your browser's localStorage. No data is sent to any external server, cloud, or third party.

✅ What we store locally on your device:
• Subject names, marks obtained, and credit hours (for GPA calculations)
• Calculated GPA and Percentage results that you choose to save
• Timestamps of when results were saved
• App preferences (if any)

🔒 How Your Data Is Used

The data stored on your device is used only for the core functionality of the app:

  • 📊 Calculating your GPA based on marks and credit hours
  • 📈 Computing percentage scores from your marks
  • 💾 Displaying your saved calculation history
  • 📚 Allowing you to manage, delete, or clear your data

🗑️ Data Control & Deletion

You have full control over all data stored by this app:

  • 🗑️ Delete individual results from the Saved Results page
  • 🧹 Clear all saved results with one click
  • 🔄 Reset subjects in the GPA Calculator at any time
  • 📱 Clear browser data – removing localStorage will erase all app data

🌐 Offline-First & No Tracking

This web app works completely offline once loaded. We do not use:

  • ❌ Analytics cookies or trackers (Google Analytics, etc.)
  • ❌ Third-party advertising networks
  • ❌ External fonts or CDNs that could log your IP
  • ❌ Any form of telemetry or crash reporting

The app uses only system fonts and local assets. Your usage remains entirely private to your browser.

👶 Children's Privacy

Our app is designed for students of all ages. Since we collect no personal information whatsoever and store nothing outside the user's device, there are no special concerns regarding children's privacy under COPPA or similar regulations.

🔧 Third-Party Services

This application does not integrate any third-party services, APIs, or external libraries that could access your data. All code runs locally in your browser. No external requests are made except for loading this page itself.

📱 LocalStorage Details

The app uses browser localStorage to persist your data between sessions. This data:

  • 🔐 Never leaves your device
  • 📁 Is isolated to this web app's origin (no other websites can access it)
  • 🧹 Can be cleared at any time via browser settings or the app's "Clear All" buttons
  • 💻 Remains until you explicitly delete it or clear browser data
💡 Tip: To completely remove all app data, go to your browser settings → Privacy → Clear site data for this website, or use the "Clear All Results" button in the Saved Results page.

📧 Contact

Since this app is fully offline and does not collect any contact information, we don't have a support server. However, if you have privacy-related questions about how this app functions, you can inspect the source code or contact the developer through the platform where you obtained this app.

By using this application, you acknowledge that all data processing occurs locally on your device and that no information is transmitted externally. You retain complete ownership and control over your data.

🔒 Privacy by design — Your data, your device, your control
